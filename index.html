About

Less is more work.

Rats, highly adaptable rodents, are found globally in urban and rural areas. Known for their intelligence and social behavior, they can transmit diseases and cause damage to property. Despite negative associations, they play ecological roles and are studied for scientific and medical research. Effective pest control is essential due to their rapid reproductive rate and potential health risks.

Rats display intelligence through problem-solving and social cooperation behaviors.

Dedicated to celebrating rats, our site promotes understanding and care. Explore rat facts, care tips, and adorable pictures. Join our community of rat lovers today and share your passion for these intelligent and affectionate creatures.
